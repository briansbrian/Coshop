# CoShop Marketplace Platform

CoShop is a marketplace platform designed to empower Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs) by providing them with an integrated online presence. The platform connects SMEs with consumers through business registration, inventory management, and geolocation-based discovery.

## Core Value Proposition

Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms that cater to large enterprises, CoShop focuses on helping SMEs compete effectively in the digital marketplace while maintaining their unique identity and local presence.

## Key Features

- **Business Management**: SME registration, verification, profile management, and staff accounts
- **Inventory System**: Real-time product catalog and inventory tracking
- **Geolocation Discovery**: Map-based browsing of shops, businesses, and services with location filtering
- **Order Management**: End-to-end order processing with status tracking
- **Integrated Delivery**: Third-party delivery service integration (Uber, Pick Up Mtaani)
- **Payment Processing**: Multi-gateway support (mobile money, credit cards, bank transfers)
- **Bidirectional Ratings**: Consumers rate SMEs; SMEs rate consumer trustworthiness
- **Messaging**: Real-time communication between consumers and SMEs
- **Analytics**: Business performance insights and data export
- **Promotions**: Discount codes and promotional campaigns

## User Types

- **SME Owners**: Manage business profiles, inventory, orders, and staff
- **Consumers**: Discover businesses, browse products, place orders, track deliveries
- **Platform Administrators**: Monitor content, moderate listings, handle reports
